About Michael Jackson

Early Life Highlights (Gary to Motown)

Born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana.

  • At just 5 years old, Michael joined his brothers to form the Jackson5.
  • By age 8, he was the group's lead singer, displaying a "soul far older than his years".
  • His father, Joe Jackson, was a demanding manager who used strict discipline - and sometimes physical punishment - to push the brothers toward success.
  • The group signed with Motown Records in 1968 (with officially debut and first releases in 1996), soon achieving four consecutive #1 hits, including "I Want You Back" (1969) and "ABC" (1970).
  • By age 11, he was a national sensation with "I Want You Back" (1969).
  • His first solo single, "Got to Be There," came in 1971 at age 13.
